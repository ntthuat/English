saw + V: thay toan bo qua trinh tu dau den cuoi
saw + V-ing: thay ngay trong luc dang nhin
seen + to-V: bi dong

I saw Tom get into his car and drive away.
I saw him looking at me.
They were seen to go out.

Mot so dong tu giac quan khac cung su dung cau truc nay:

_ hear - heard 
_ feel - felt
_ notice - noticed
_ listen to
_ smell
_ find - found
